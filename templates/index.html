Spanning 5.33km in length, the highly-anticipated Dublin City Grand Prix will finally take place in the Summer of 2026! With 3 DRS zones and 20 turns, undoubtedly plenty of action will happen out on track, as 20 of the world's best drivers battle to become the first ever Dublin City Grand Prix winner.

Located in the heart of the city just south of the Liffey, the race will see drivers pass famous sights, such as Temple Bar, Christchurch and St. Stephen's Green, before finishing the lap on College Green in front of Trinity College.

To view the street track mapped out to the city, please click on the race track diagram with the stands. Alternatively, if you want to return to the image of the track layout with the stands, click on the Google Map image.
